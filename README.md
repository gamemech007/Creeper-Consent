Creeper Consent adds a funny and creepy twist to Minecraft’s most iconic mob.

Instead of instantly exploding, creepers now pause and ask for your permission through a mysterious cryptic GUI. When a creeper is about to explode near you, a dark green creeper-themed screen appears with two choices: allow the explosion or deny it.

If you allow it, the creeper continues and explodes normally. If you deny it, the creeper is removed safely.

Features:

• Creepers ask before exploding • Creepy green cryptic consent GUI • Allow or deny each explosion • Denying removes the creeper • Lightweight and simple • Fun vanilla-friendly joke mod

This mod is perfect for players who want a humorous, cursed, or chaotic twist to survival gameplay while keeping the creeper experience recognizable.
